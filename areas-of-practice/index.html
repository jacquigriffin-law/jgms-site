Areas of practice

Practical legal support across four core service areas, with flexible appointment options for Sydney, Parramatta and Darwin clients.

👪

Family law

Support with separation, divorce, parenting arrangements, child support, property settlement and related family law matters. Jacqui offers fixed-fee packages and unbundled services to help make family law affordable for working people.

She holds NSW Legal Aid panels for family law and the s102NA cross-examination scheme, and appears at Federal Circuit and Family Court registries around Australia.

Family law services →
🛡️

Care and protection

Help for parents, carers and family members involved in child protection matters and Children's Court proceedings. These matters move quickly — early advice on documents, expectations and next steps can make a significant difference.

Jacqui holds NSW Legal Aid panels for care and protection matters and has experience across Children's Court proceedings in NSW and the NT.

Care and protection →
⚖️

Criminal law and AVOs

Advice and representation for criminal matters, domestic violence-related proceedings and apprehended violence orders. Phone and video appointments mean urgent first-step advice is available quickly when timing matters.

Jacqui holds the NSW Legal Aid domestic violence panel.

Criminal law and AVOs →
📜

Wills and estates

Wills, probate, powers of attorney, appointments of enduring guardianship and estate administration. Planning ahead protects both you and the people who depend on you.

Support is also available for executors navigating probate, estate debts and distributions.

Getting Started

Choosing the right first step

Not every matter requires the same approach. Some situations need urgent action; others benefit from early information gathering and careful planning.

An initial appointment can help clarify immediate priorities, the likely process, and the most practical path forward for your specific circumstances. Jacqui is direct about what is needed, what can wait, and roughly what the process will involve.
