Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to come to your office?

No — we can come to you, or you can meet via Zoom, FaceTime, or phone. We also have offices in Parramatta and Darwin if you prefer face-to-face.

Yes, we accept Legal Aid grants for eligible matters including family law, care and protection, and criminal law.

We offer competitive rates and can discuss payment options during your initial consultation. Book online to get started.
