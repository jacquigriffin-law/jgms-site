Phone, video and online options

Get legal advice and keep your matter moving without having to travel to a physical office — by phone, video or secure online booking.

Virtual and Remote Options

Designed for convenience

If you prefer to communicate primarily online or by phone, that can often be arranged. Remote appointments reduce friction, travel time and delay without compromising the quality of advice.

Jacqui uses a range of tools depending on what works best for the client and the matter:

  • Phone consultations — straightforward for most advice matters
  • Zoom and FaceTime video calls for face-to-face appointments
  • Email for correspondence and document exchange
  • Electronic signing of documents where appropriate
  • Secure online booking via LawTap for initial and follow-up appointments

When phone or video works well

Remote appointments are suitable for:

  • Initial consultations and advice
  • Reviewing documents and correspondence
  • Discussing strategy and next steps
  • Estate planning and will instructions
  • Follow-up appointments after initial in-person meetings
  • Clients based outside Parramatta or Darwin

Some matters may still require in-person attendance for court, signing or witnessing purposes. This will be advised where relevant.
