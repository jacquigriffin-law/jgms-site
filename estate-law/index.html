Wills & Estates

Planning and administration support for wills, probate, powers of attorney and estate matters.

Planning and protection

Estate planning is about making your wishes clear and protecting the people who depend on you.

Support is available for wills, powers of attorney, enduring guardianship and more complex estate planning where a standard form document may not be suitable.

  • Wills and estate planning
  • Probate and letters of administration
  • Powers of attorney
  • Appointments of enduring guardianship
  • Executor and estate administration support

When legal help matters

  • Blended families or second relationships
  • Business interests or trusts
  • Executor duties and probate steps
  • Questions about capacity, witnessing or validity

Executor support

Executors may need help locating assets, arranging valuations, applying for probate, handling liabilities and making distributions.

Reviewing your will

A will should be reviewed after major life events such as marriage, divorce, significant asset changes or family changes.

General information only

The right document structure depends on personal circumstances. Estate planning should be tailored carefully rather than assumed from a generic template.
