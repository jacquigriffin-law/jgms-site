Fee Structures

How fees may be structured

Fixed-fee work

Best suited to work with a predictable scope — document preparation, consent orders, some divorce applications and other defined tasks. You know the cost before work begins.

Unbundled legal services

Pay only for specific help you need — such as document review, advice on a particular issue, or representation at a single hearing. Designed to help self-represented litigants who cannot afford full-service representation.

Stage-based pricing

Some matters can be broken into stages so you understand the likely cost of each step before moving forward. Helps manage costs in longer-running matters.

Tailored estimates

Contested or urgent matters usually require tailored costs advice after the first appointment. The eventual scope cannot be predicted accurately at the outset, and Jacqui will be direct about this.

Costs transparency

Jacqui aims to give clear costs information before you commit to any work. Additional charges such as court filing fees, barrister fees and expert reports are generally separate to professional fees and will be explained clearly at your first appointment.
