About Jacqui Griffin Mobile Solicitor

A legal practice built around flexibility, accessibility and practical support for clients in Sydney, Parramatta and Darwin.

A practice designed around access

Jacqui Griffin Mobile Solicitor combines established legal experience with a service model that works for modern clients.

The practice offers phone, video, in-person and suitable mobile appointments so clients can get help in a way that is realistic for work, care responsibilities, court deadlines and distance.

Clients often come to the practice during stressful periods. The aim is to provide clear communication, practical next steps and a professional approach that keeps matters moving.

Flexible by design

Modern service delivery
