About Jacqui Griffin Mobile Solicitor

Jacqui Griffin Mobile Solicitor offers a mobile service, a virtual law experience and face-to-face conferences in Parramatta and Darwin.

A practice designed around access

The aim is to provide a quality legal service that is convenient, more affordable, quicker and simpler.

If you wish to use the virtual law or online law option to communicate primarily in your matter, the technology is available through secure online booking, video conferencing via Zoom, messenger, FaceTime, electronic signing of documents and phone calls.

Jacqui Griffin appears in a number of different courts in NSW and the NT, and the service model recognises that many clients need flexibility about how and where they meet with their solicitor.

Flexible by design

Mobile, virtual and in-person options to suit the circumstances of the client and the matter.

What this means in practice

Mobile service
