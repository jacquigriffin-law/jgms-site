Care & Protection

Support for families facing child protection and Children's Court related issues.

Clear guidance through difficult situations

Care and protection matters are often urgent, highly stressful and deeply personal. The focus is on helping clients understand the process, prepare properly and respond to the issues raised.

  • Children's Court matters
  • Case planning and preparation
  • Communication around interim and final arrangements
  • Support for parents, carers and family members

How support can help

These matters usually move quickly. Early advice can assist with documents, expectations, court preparation and keeping the focus on the practical next step.
